Our History

Welcome to The Bake House, where every loaf tells a story. Our journey began in 2001, when our founder, Jack Jhons, discovered a passion for baking in the heart of Kandy, Sri Lanka. What started as a humble kitchen experiment soon blossomed into a beloved neighborhood bakery.

Over the years,The Bake House has become a cherished part of the community, known for our commitment to quality and taste. We use traditional recipes passed down through generations, combined with the finest ingredients sourced locally, to create breads and pastries that delight the senses.

From our signature sourdough loaves to delicate French pastries, each creation is crafted with care and a deep appreciation for the art of baking. Our goal is simple: to bring joy to our customers through every bite.
